# My Favorite Movie: *Inception* (2010)

**Inception** is a mind-bending thriller directed by *Christopher Nolan*. It stars *Leonardo DiCaprio* as Dom Cobb, a skilled "extractor" who enters the dreams of others to steal secrets.

## Plot Overview

The film follows Cobb and his team as they attempt an **"inception"** — planting an idea into someone's mind without them realizing it. The goal is to make the target believe the idea was their own.
